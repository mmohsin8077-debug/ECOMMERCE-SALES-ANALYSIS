# ECOMMERCE-SALES-ANALYSIS
Analyze e-commerce sales data to identify top-performing products,
customers, regions, and business trends using SQL.
- SQL
- Joins
- CTEs
- Window Functions
- Aggregate Functions
- Subqueries
- Business Analysis

#QUERIES
1. Find the total sales, total profit, and total number of orders.
2. Find the top 10 products generating the highest sales.
3.Show total sales and profit for each region.
4. Find how many orders were placed using each payment mode.
5. Calculate average discount offered in each category.
6. Top 5 Customers by Revenue
7. Calculate total sales for every month.
8. Find the city with highest total sales.
9. Calculate each category's contribution to total sales.
10. Find products whose total quantity sold is greater than average quantity sold across all products.
11. What are the top-performing and lowest-performing products within each distinct business category based on total generated profit?"
12. Which specific product ranks as the second-highest sales generator within each individual product category, and how far behind is it from the top performer?
13. #What is the Month-over-Month (MoM) sales growth rate.
14. Find customers whose total sales are greater than the average customer sales.

#KEY INSIGHTS
- Electronics generated 45% of total revenue.
- Credit Card was the most used payment mode.
- Top 20% products contributed approximately 80% of sales.
- West region generated the highest profit.
